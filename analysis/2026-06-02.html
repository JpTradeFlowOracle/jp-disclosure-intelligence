Yamadai corrects financial results unit in tender offer disclosure.

Yamadai has issued a correction to its previously disclosed financial results for March 2026, which were included in materials related to a tender offer by Nice Co., Ltd. The correction primarily changes the unit of currency for all reported figures from thousand yen to million yen, drastically altering the perceived scale of the company's financial performance.

This correction is highly significant for investors, as it rectifies a substantial misrepresentation of Yamadai's financial scale within the tender offer documentation. The true magnitude of the company's revenue and losses is 1,000 times larger than initially stated, directly impacting any valuation analysis for the ongoing tender offer.

  • Correction issued on June 2, 2026, for information originally disclosed on June 1, 2026.
  • The correction applies to the consolidated financial results for the fiscal year ended March 2026.
  • Revenue was corrected from 4,102 thousand yen to 4,102 million yen.
  • Operating loss was corrected from 286 thousand yen to 286 million yen.
  • Ordinary loss was corrected from 263 thousand yen to 263 million yen.
  • Net loss attributable to parent company shareholders was corrected from 280 thousand yen to 280 million yen.
Investor Takeaway Investors must note that Yamadai's financial figures, including revenue and losses, are 1,000 times larger than initially presented in the tender offer materials, requiring a re-evaluation of the company's financial standing.
